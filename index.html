What is the Fibonacci Spiral?

The Fibonacci spiral is a pattern that approximates the golden spiral, and it is created by drawing arcs connecting the opposite corners of squares in the Fibonacci tiling. It is named after the Fibonacci sequence, where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones.

Applications

  • Architecture
  • Nature (shells, flowers)
  • Art and Design
  • Stock Market Analysis

Mathematics of the Fibonacci Spiral
